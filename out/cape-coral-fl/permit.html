SCOPE OF WORK
INSTALL A WHOLE-HOME BACKUP ENERGY STORAGE SYSTEM (BATTERY ONLY, NO PV) CONSISTING OF (02) ENPHASE IQ BATTERY 5P UNITS AND (01) ENPHASE IQ SYSTEM CONTROLLER 3G AT THE OWNER RESIDENCE LOCATED AT 5217 SW 11TH AVE, CAPE CORAL, FL 33914. THE SYSTEM INTERCONNECTS WITH THE UTILITY GRID THROUGH THE EXISTING 200A ELECTRICAL SERVICE. ALL EQUIPMENT IS OUTDOOR RATED (NEMA 3R) AND WALL-MOUNTED ON THE EAST EXTERIOR WALL ADJACENT TO THE EXISTING SERVICE EQUIPMENT.

ENERGY STORAGE SYSTEM NOTES
1. ALL EQUIPMENT SHALL BE LISTED AND LABELED FOR ITS APPLICATION. IQ BATTERY 5P IS UL 9540 CERTIFIED; INSTALL PER MANUFACTURER LISTING AND INSTALLATION MANUAL.
2. MAINTAIN 36" DEPTH X 30" WIDTH WORKING CLEARANCE IN FRONT OF ALL ELECTRICAL EQUIPMENT PER NEC 110.26.
3. BATTERY UNITS WALL-MOUNTED WITH BOTTOM OF ENCLOSURE 30" ABOVE FINISHED GRADE, MINIMUM 12" CLEAR BETWEEN UNITS.
4. INTERCONNECTION VIA IQ SYSTEM CONTROLLER 3G (SERVICE ENTRANCE RATED, 200A). UTILITY GRID FORMS THE PRIMARY SOURCE; SYSTEM PROVIDES WHOLE-HOME BACKUP ON GRID LOSS.
5. VERIFY (E) GROUNDING ELECTRODE SYSTEM AT MSP. IF NOT ACCESSIBLE OR VERIFIABLE, INSTALL A NEW 5/8" X 8' GROUND ROD AND BOND EQUIPMENT GROUNDING ACCORDINGLY.
6. ALL OUTDOOR CONDUIT AND FITTINGS SHALL BE RATED FOR WET LOCATIONS. SEAL ALL WALL PENETRATIONS WATERTIGHT.
7. SMOKE AND CO ALARMS SHALL COMPLY WITH FBC-R R314/R315 WHERE APPLICABLE.
8. DRAWINGS ARE DIAGRAMMATIC. FIELD CONDITIONS PREVAIL; REPORT DISCREPANCIES TO THE CONTRACTOR BEFORE COMMENCING WORK.
